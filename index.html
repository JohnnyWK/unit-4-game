The Crystal Game

Enjoy Some Crystalized Tunes While You Play...

It's everyone's favorite American Pastime THE CRYSTAL GAME! You probably already know the rules, who doesn't? Just in case you don't, though, here are the rules:
  • You will be given a goal number, randomized at the beginning of every game.
  • You have 4 crystals, each with a random, unknown value given at the beginning of each game.
  • When you select a crystal, the value of that crystal is added to your number.
  • Match the goal number exactly, DON'T GO OVER, to win!
